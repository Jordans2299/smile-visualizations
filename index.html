SMILE.

An exploration of the World Happiness Index

View Proposal/Process Book Here

The first section goes here, and may require scrolling. It will perform statistical analysis on the factors of happiness. It will do the following

  • Examine the factors that the most happy and least happy countries say are the biggest contributors to their happiness--this is self reported information on the Gallup World Poll
  • It will then examine the real correlation between happiness scores and metrics we studied using statistical analysis. This will allow us to see what factors are objectively most important--the most common markers--and will allow us to see whether or not self-reported happiness contributors seem to reflect what is actually making people happy.
